Let’s read about some technological wonders of the 21st Century.

Invented in 2006, Blu-ray changed the way data was recorded and stored due to its large storage capacity and high-resolution imagery which took movie-viewing to a whole new level.

Launched in 2001, the Segway is a two-wheeled scooter that self-balances and runs on super-efficient batteries that actually charge when going downhill or slowing down! It uses a gyroscope to sense subtle body movements and uses this information to change speed and direction.

Watching a movie is fun enough, but 3D television took the experience to new heights, adding a sensory and immersive experience for the viewer, almost making them feel like the movie is happening around them.

Drones are unmanned, remote-operated flying vehicles. They have been used in warfare, to supply emergency aid to unreachable areas, for pesticide spraying, and for taking aerial photos and videos.

If you love reading books, you know they’re heavy and take up space. eReaders gained popularity around 2004 and have made reading more environment friendly, apart from making multiple books accessible for reading on a device that is a fraction of the weight of the actual books that it contains.

68

The wireless touch mouse was invented in 2000. It requires no wire and can be used by tapping the surface, much like a phone screen.

Virtual keyboards are presently used in tablets and phones, making the size of devices much smaller and more portable. The next step in virtual keyboards is the laser keyboards that will use sensing technology to let you type on a keyboard that seemingly isn’t there!

YouTube was launched in 2005 and created video-based social media. It also became the first media site which did not create its own content and gave rise to the world of vlogging or video blogging.

3D printers create true-size 3D objects from scanned pictures. The possibilities of 3D printing are endless and still being explored—from everyday objects to organ transplants, food, small mechanical parts, and even houses!
