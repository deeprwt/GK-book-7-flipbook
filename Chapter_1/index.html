Plants give us oxygen, shade, and provide food. But did you know that some can be deadly? Here are five such poisonous plants.

We know that smoking cigarettes and chewing tobacco causes cancer. All parts of the tobacco plant are highly poisonous and can prove to be fatal if consumed.

Oleander is a popular shrub grown for its colourful flowers. This plant is so poisonous that honey made from bees that feed on it can cause nausea. Ingesting any part of the plant causes death.

Water Hemlock, a pretty and delicate-looking plant from the carrot family causes convulsions, nausea, and even death if eaten.

Daffodil flowers, with their pretty yellow colour, are associated with spring. However, the bulbs of these popular flowers are fatal if eaten by dogs.
