14. War of words

Choose the correct option to answer these questions on vocabulary and other aspects related to the English language.

1. A synonym for ‘salutation’ is:

a) greeting b) parade c) dawn

2. What is the antonym for ‘abundant’?

a) overflowing b) plentiful c) meagre

3. Which of these words means ‘tighten’?

a) consign b) conduct c) constrict

4. What is the name of the theatre that Shakespeare’s company built in 1599?

a) Globe Theatre b) Gielgud Theatre c) Royal Albert Hall

5. How many lines does a ballad stanza contain?

a) eight b) four c) three

6. Which author is the first Indian citizen to win the Booker Prize?

a) Salman Rushdie b) Kiran Desai c) Arundhati Roy

7. Expressing oneself using words that mean the opposite is called:

a) irony b) sarcasm c) parody

8. A synonym for the verb ‘to lollygag’ is:

a) to eat b) to laugh c) to be idle

9. A sentence that uses every letter in the English language is called:

a) a palindrome b) a pangram c) a rebus

10. So-so, net-net, tom-tom, are examples of words or terms made by repeating the same word. What are these types of words called?

a) tautonym b) homonyms c) antigram

A.
