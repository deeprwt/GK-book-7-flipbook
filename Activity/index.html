In 2017, the Government of India introduced the Goods and Services Tax or GST. GST replaces multiple taxes with one simple tax, making it easier for the consumer. The government then distributes the money made from GST to the various authorities and departments.

There are two main types of GST. The GST levied by the centre is Central GST (CGST) and that by the states is State GST (SGST). The total GST is equally divided into CGST and SGST.
