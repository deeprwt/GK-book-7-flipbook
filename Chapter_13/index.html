13. Blast from the past

Choose the correct option to answer these questions on ancient cities, civilisations, customs, and cultures.

1. Sobekneferu gained immense power and regalia when she became the first female pharaoh of this ancient civilisation in 1806 bc.

a) Mesopotamian

b) Byzantine

c) Egyptian

2. In 1362, King Henry III ordered English to be used in British courts because it was the language of commoners. Before this, the language spoken at courts and by the British elite was:

a) French b) Latin c) German

3. Known for using their sharp swords, they were military nobility and a high-ranking class of warriors in medieval Japan.

a) Yakuza

b) Shogun

c) Samurai

4. The Parliament of this country was established in 930 ce and is the oldest continuously acting parliament in the world.

a) Iceland

b) Thailand

c) Mongolia

5. Dating back to at least 11,000 years, it is considered the oldest continuously inhabited city in the world. In 2008, it was named the Arab Capital of Culture.

a) Baghdad b) Mecca c) Damascus

A.

Did you know

In ancient Egypt, the papyrus plant was used to make numerous items like paper, mats, and even fabric! Paper made from the papyrus plant is very strong and hence many scriptures written on it have remained well preserved for over thousands of years.
